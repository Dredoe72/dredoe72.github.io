About CutzByDre

My haircuts are tailored for the male who appreciates premium quality, time and a flawless look.

It all started in a dorm room my freshman year at MSU. I had some clippers that my father sent with me to keep myself groomed. Good friend of mine saw me one day standing in the mirror trying to groom my facial hairs and asked "Can you line me up..." The rest is history....
